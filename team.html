Lydiah Sereti

Lydiah Sereti the visionary founder of our organization, plays a pivotal role in connecting with marginalized and vulnerable communities. She is responsible for identifying their unique needs and ensuring that our services reach those who need them most. Mary's tireless efforts are instrumental in promoting inclusivity and support. Lisa Mogambi

Lisa Mogambi is a passionate advocate for the rights and well-being of individuals with disabilities. With a background in healthcare, she serves as the nurse, overseeing all medical services and ensuring that they are tailored to meet the unique needs of our diverse community. Ms. Lisa is committed to creating an inclusive and accessible healthcare environment.

Benjamin

Mr Benjamin holds the vital role of Sign Language Coordinator. He is an ICT officer as well as a PLWD who is a deaf and understands their needs and language He has been at the center of our operations since inception. He ensures that our medical facilities are fully accessible to the deaf community, providing sign language interpretation and training to healthcare staff to guarantee effective communication and understanding.

Faith Meroka Gekengo

Ms. Faith Meroka Gekengo is our Nutrition Specialist, focusing on the crucial area of nutrition. She ensures that our communities have access to well-balanced diets, promoting health and well-being. Rebecca's expertise plays a pivotal role in improving overall nutrition within the community.

Mariam Manwa

Mariam Manwa plays a crucial role as our Social Support Worker. She provides emotional support, counseling, and social services to individuals and families facing challenging circumstances. Her work fosters a supportive and caring environment for our community. She also does sensitization and awareness creation on PHC at community level.

Abel Ouru

Abel Ouru, our Director of Agriculture and advisor, is a seasoned agricultural expert. He's dedicated to providing adaptive farming solutions and empowering individuals with disabilities to become self-reliant through innovative agricultural practices. Abel's leadership ensures that our agriculture programs are both effective and sustainable.
